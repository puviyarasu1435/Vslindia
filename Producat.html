PULTRUDED GRATING WALKWAY

VSL offers a wide range of FRP pultruded grating to fulfill various needs of customers. FRP grating offers unmatched benefits in corrosion resistance, high strength to weight ratio, zero maintenance and case of installation, non-conductive nature and possesses anti-skid design and UV resistance.


A State-of-the-art manufacturing facility produces pultruded profiles (Pultrusion profiles- ‘I’ section for producing horizontal and vertical walkway for solar rooftops)


ADVANTAGES OF PULTRUDED WALKWAY


  • Very high strength to weight ratio
  • UV Resistant
  • Non corrosive
  • Ease of installation
  • Very long life
  • Customization possible (depending upon the Width needed)
  • Anti-skid Design
  • Walkways have less than one third the weight of steel.
